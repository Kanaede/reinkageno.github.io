Seeing me cry like a classmate who was bullied at school, they really looked forward to it. Yeah... I couldn't even feel anything when it happened, just like normal pain, then dying and being unconscious, it all happened without compassion.

Come on, I still have a lot of endurance, finish it off until it's just one or empty it, I'll enjoy it while I'm unconscious.
